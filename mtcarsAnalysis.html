Motor Trend Cars Analysis

S. Kairs

October 13, 2014

In this report we investigate the relationship between transmission type and fuel efficiency, as reported in the 1974 Motor Trend Us magazine. This dataset consists of fuel consumption and 10 aspects of automobile design and performance for 32 automobiles (1973–74 models).[1] The report is prepared as part of the [Regression Models][courselink] course, offered by [Johns Hopkins University][jhu] on [Coursera][coursera].
